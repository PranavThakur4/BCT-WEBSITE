About

BCT Aerospace

Defense and Aerospace Component Manufacturing and Assembly Company Balaji CNC Technologies (BCT) is a Defence and Aerospace manufacturing company established in 2007. The first unit was built in Hyderabad with an aim to boost manufacturing sector in India. BCT still holds its roots strong and believes in quality thus is an ISO 9001: 2015 and for its safety ISO 45001:2018 certified. BCT produces precision metallic components and delivers it to the leading aerospace firms in India.

BCT’s strongest driving force has been their Managing Partner Mr. Balaji who has been in this field since the past 25 years. He has worked in the Singapore and has a vision to implement such advanced technology in India as well.
